# Product Strategy

> This document explains who we're building for, why they'll choose Atlas, and how we'll grow the product into a sustainable business.

---

# Product Vision

Build the easiest platform for organizations to manage people, attendance, follow-ups, and engagement in one place.

---

# Our Mission

Help organizations stop managing spreadsheets and start building stronger relationships with the people they serve.

---

# The Problem

Organizations use too many disconnected tools.

For example:

- Excel for contacts
- Paper for attendance
- WhatsApp for communication
- Sticky notes for follow-ups
- Different apps for events

This leads to:

- Missed follow-ups
- Duplicate work
- Poor visibility
- Lost information
- Low engagement

Atlas brings everything together.

---

# Our Target Market

Atlas is built for organizations that manage people.

### Phase 1 (Primary Market)

- Churches

Why?

- Large market
- High need for follow-up
- Attendance is important
- Easy to validate ideas
- Strong referral potential

---

### Phase 2

- NGOs

- Communities

---

### Phase 3

- Businesses
- Healthcare
- Associations
- Event organizers

---

# Ideal Customer

Our first ideal customer is:

A church with:

- 100–2,000 members
- Multiple workers
- Regular events
- Manual attendance process
- Difficulty tracking visitors and follow-ups

---

# Value Proposition

Atlas helps organizations manage everything about their people from one platform.

Instead of switching between spreadsheets, WhatsApp, notebooks, and different software, everything lives in one place.

Users always know:

- Who their people are
- What happened
- What needs attention
- What should happen next

# Revenue Model

Atlas will use a subscription model.

## Free

Suitable for small organizations.

Includes:

- One organization
- Basic people management
- Events
- Attendance
- Limited reports

---

## Pro

Designed for growing organizations.

Includes:

- Unlimited members
- Advanced reports
- Follow-up management
- Journey tracking
- Multiple admins
- Custom branding
- Export tools

---

## Business

For larger organizations.

Includes:

- Multiple branches
- Team management
- Advanced permissions
- Automation
- API access
- Priority support

---

## Enterprise

Custom pricing.

Includes:

- Dedicated support
- Custom onboarding
- Custom integrations
- SLA
- Enterprise security

---

# Pricing Philosophy

Keep pricing simple.

Customers should understand pricing in less than one minute.

Avoid confusing feature comparisons.

Charge based on the value delivered.

# Strategy Principles

Every decision should answer:

- Does this solve a real customer problem?
- Is it simple to understand?
- Will customers pay for it?
- Can it scale?
- Does it fit our long-term vision?

If the answer is "No", we rethink the decision.
