# Logo Strategy

## Purpose

This document defines the strategic direction for the Relvio logo.

The logo should communicate the brand's purpose, values, and vision while remaining timeless, scalable, and recognizable.

It should work equally well across web, mobile, print, presentations, merchandise, and social media.

---

# Brand Summary

Relvio is a People Operating System that helps organizations manage people, relationships, communication, attendance, events, and growth.

The logo should represent connection, organization, trust, and progress.

---

# Logo Objectives

The logo should be:

- Memorable
- Simple
- Timeless
- Modern
- Scalable
- Distinctive

It should be recognizable even without the product name.

---

# Brand Personality

The logo should feel:

- Professional
- Intelligent
- Friendly
- Reliable
- Premium
- Modern

Avoid looking overly corporate or playful.

---

# Core Brand Ideas

The logo should subtly communicate one or more of the following:

- Relationships
- Connection
- Growth
- Community
- Organization
- Unity
- Trust
- Collaboration
- Progress

Avoid literal representations.

The concept should be symbolic rather than descriptive.

---

# Logo Style

Preferred style:

- Minimal
- Geometric
- Flat
- Balanced
- Modern

Avoid:

- 3D effects
- Complex illustrations
- Heavy gradients
- Clip-art styles
- Excessive details

---

# Logo Structure

Relvio should have:

## Primary Logo

Icon + Wordmark

---

## Secondary Logo

Horizontal version

---

## Icon Only

For:

- App Icon
- Favicon
- Social Media
- Mobile

---

## Wordmark Only

For branding where the icon is unnecessary.

---

# Scalability

The logo should remain recognizable at:

- 16px
- 24px
- 48px
- 128px
- Large signage

No important details should disappear at small sizes.

---

# Color Usage

Primary:

Atlas Blue (Brand Primary)

Support:

White

Dark Gray

Monochrome versions should also be available.

---

# Typography

Wordmark should use a clean geometric sans-serif typeface.

Characteristics:

- Modern
- Readable
- Balanced
- Slightly rounded

The typography should feel approachable while remaining professional.

---

# Shape Language

Preferred characteristics:

- Rounded corners
- Smooth curves
- Clean geometry
- Consistent proportions

The symbol should feel stable and balanced.

---

# Symbol Direction

The symbol should be abstract.

It may subtly suggest:

- Connected nodes
- Network
- Growth
- Community
- Motion
- Unity
- Flow

The symbol should not rely on obvious industry clichés.

---

# App Icon

The icon should work independently from the wordmark.

Requirements:

- Recognizable
- Simple
- Balanced
- Easy to identify on mobile devices

---

# Responsive Logo

Create variations for:

- Light backgrounds
- Dark backgrounds
- Small sizes
- Large formats

---

# Accessibility

Maintain sufficient contrast.

Ensure readability on:

- White
- Dark
- Colored backgrounds

---

# Brand Consistency

Never:

- Stretch the logo
- Rotate the logo
- Change proportions
- Add effects
- Change colors outside the approved palette

---

# Long-Term Vision

The logo should remain relevant for at least the next 10 years.

It should support future products within the Relvio ecosystem.

Examples:

- Relvio Forms
- Relvio Events
- Relvio Chat
- Relvio Analytics
- Relvio Learn

The identity should scale naturally across all products.

---

# Success Criteria

The logo is successful when:

- It is instantly recognizable.
- It reflects the Relvio brand.
- It works across all platforms.
- It remains simple and memorable.
- It feels premium and timeless.
