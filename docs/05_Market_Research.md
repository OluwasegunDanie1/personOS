# Market Research

## Purpose

Understand the market, validate the problem, and identify where Atlas fits.

---

# The Problem

Organizations of all sizes struggle to manage people effectively.

Common challenges include:

- Attendance tracking
- Follow-up management
- Scattered information
- Poor reporting
- Lack of accountability
- Manual processes

Most organizations solve these problems using multiple tools instead of one platform.

---

# Current Solutions

Many organizations currently use:

- Excel
- Google Sheets
- WhatsApp
- Paper records
- Google Forms
- Microsoft Forms
- Basic CRMs

This works initially but becomes difficult as the organization grows.

---

# Market Trends

Organizations are increasingly adopting cloud software because it offers:

- Remote access
- Automatic backups
- Collaboration
- Lower maintenance
- Better reporting

There is also growing demand for mobile-first applications.
