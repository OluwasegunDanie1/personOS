# Product Blueprint

> This document explains what we're building, who it's for, and why it exists.

---

# Product Summary

Atlas is a cloud-based platform that helps organizations manage people, attendance, follow-ups, events, communication, and engagement from one place.

Instead of using multiple apps and spreadsheets, organizations can manage everything through a single platform.

Our goal is simple:

> Help organizations build better relationships with the people they serve.

---

# The Problem

Today, many organizations work like this:

- Contacts are stored in Excel.
- Attendance is recorded on paper.
- Follow-ups happen on WhatsApp.
- Events are managed separately.
- Reports take hours to prepare.

As the organization grows, it becomes harder to know:

- Who is active?
- Who needs a follow-up?
- Who stopped attending?
- What happened last week?
- What should happen next?

Information becomes scattered, and people are easily forgotten.

---

# Our Solution

Atlas brings everything together.

Instead of managing different tools, organizations manage one platform.

Everything revolves around people.

Every interaction is recorded.

Every follow-up is tracked.

Every event is connected.

Every team works from the same information.

---

# Who We're Building For

Atlas is designed for organizations that manage people.

Examples include:

- Churches
- Schools
- NGOs
- Businesses
- Communities
- Training Centres
- Event Organizers
- Healthcare Organizations

The platform should be flexible enough to support different industries without changing the core system.

---

# Our Core Philosophy

People are not just records.

Every person is on a journey.

Our software should help organizations understand that journey and know what to do next.

---

# Core Modules

## 👥 People

Store and manage everyone in the organization.

Examples:

- Members
- Visitors
- Customers
- Students
- Staff
- Volunteers

---

## 📅 Events

Create and manage events.

Examples:

- Sunday Service
- Training
- Conference
- Workshop
- Meeting

---

## ✅ Attendance

Track attendance for any event.

Support:

- QR Code
- Manual Check-in
- Self Check-in
- Bulk Check-in

---

## 📝 Follow-up

Create follow-up tasks.

Example:

Visitor attended for the first time.

↓

Assign follow-up to Worker A.

↓

Reminder sent.

↓

Status updated.

---

## 📞 Communication

Communicate with people from one place.

Future support:

- Email
- SMS
- WhatsApp
- Push Notifications

---

## 📊 Reports

Provide useful insights.

Examples:

- Attendance trends
- Visitor retention
- Growth reports
- Follow-up completion
- Event performance

# Product Goals

We want Atlas to help organizations:

- Stay organized
- Save time
- Improve follow-up
- Increase engagement
- Make better decisions

---

# MVP

Version 1 should focus on solving one problem well.

The MVP includes:

- Authentication
- Organization Setup
- People Management
- Events
- Attendance
- Follow-up
- Dashboard
- Reports
- Roles & Permissions

Anything else can wait.

---

# What Atlas Is NOT

Atlas is not:

- An accounting system
- A payroll system
- A social media platform
- A video conferencing tool

Those can integrate later if needed.

---

# Success Looks Like...

A church can know which first-time visitors haven't been contacted.

A school can see student attendance instantly.

An NGO can track beneficiaries without spreadsheets.

A business can monitor customer engagement from one dashboard.

If organizations stop relying on Excel and scattered tools because of Atlas, we've succeeded.

---

# Product Principles

Every feature should answer these questions:

- Does it solve a real problem?
- Is it simple to use?
- Will people actually use it?
- Does it fit our long-term vision?

If the answer is "No," we rethink the feature.

---

# Looking Ahead

Atlas is being built as a platform.

The first release solves today's problems.

Future releases will introduce:

- Automation
- Workflow Builder
- AI Insights
- Public API
- Integrations
- Marketplace
- Mobile Apps

But first, we build a product people love.
